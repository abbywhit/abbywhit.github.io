BIO

Carl, hails from Toronto, Canada. As the son of a DJ, Carl has always been exposed to a wide variety of music. This diversity of music is apparent when listening to Carl’s eclectic blends on the dance floor. With his vast knowledge of music and ability to read crowds, Carl knows exactly which classics will get everyone on their feet dancing!

Carl has over 9 years of DJ experience and loves what he does! It is his passion to make sure that you are at ease and people are on the dance floor. Carl will coordinate with you and your other vendors so you can relax and enjoy your event. His equipment is always new and up to date with a setup that is sleek and modern.

Carl is more than just a DJ; he’s your host, setting an ambiance and guiding you and your guests through the entire event.

Carl is looking forward to getting the party started! Let's have some fun with your new favorite DJ!
